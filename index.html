Dimitrios Stamoulis

I am a PI Research Associate (Special Faculty) with the Dept. of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at The University of Texas at Austin. Before that, I was a Principal Research Manager at Microsoft (2020-2024), where I founded and led the CoStrategist R&D Group. I received a PhD in ECE from Carnegie Mellon University under the supervision of Diana Marculescu. I received a MEng in ECE from McGill University, Montreal, and a Diploma in ECE from NTUA, Athens.
